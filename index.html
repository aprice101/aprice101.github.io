Inorganic Synthetic Chemistry - From the main group to the actinides

About me

I am currently a Broom McIntyre Fellow and PI of the Price Group in the School of Chemistry at the University of St Andrews. Previously I was an education focused associate lecturer in inorganic chemistry at the University of St Andrews, with an interest in increasing the amount and quality of interactive digital learning resources available to undergraduate students. Before this, I was a postdoc conducting research into the properties of the f-block elements at Lawrence Berkeley National Laboratory and University of California Berkeley in Prof. P. Arnold's group. I obtained my PhD in 2018 after working with Dr. Michael Cowley at the University of Edinburgh on reactive phosphorus and boron containing molecules.

Research Interests

My research interests focus on obtaining functional organometallic complexes through ligand design: two broad areas I am interested in are low coordinate f-element complexes and ligand design for improved separation of the lanthanoids.
